What to do

Try out our huge range of activities and tours, go quad biking, zip lining, try out a range of water sports or simply releax on the beach. It really is up to you.

Island tour

Tour the whole island of Sal, visit shark bay, the salt lakes and experience the blue eye. After all have a relaxing meal and drink in a local restaurant.

Zip lining

Speeds of up to 80 mph on a zip line. Enjoy the singing and dancing with our local tour guides.
